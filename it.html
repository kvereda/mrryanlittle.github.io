Information Technology Industry (IT)


What is IT?
          The IT industry involves hardware, software, data, telecom, and social media.
        Two of the biggest names in IT are Apple Inc., and Alphabet. Apple is one of the
        founding companies of the personal computer, and Alphabet is the parent companny
        of Google. As of Nov. 16, 2020, Apple has a market cap of over $2T. As of
        Nov. 16, 2020, Alphabet has a market cap of over $1.2T.
What has been the effect of 2020?
          The training predictions could not predict the effect of 2020. Both companies,
        as IT industry indicators, show that in early 2020 stock prices dropped dramaticaly.
        The prices recovered quickly and increased well beyond predictions. The likely
        reason for this is the COVID-19 pandemic and more people spending time at home
        for both work and entertainment.
