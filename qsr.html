Quick Service Restaurant Industry (QSR)


What is QSR?
          The Quick Service Restaurant industry, also known as fast food,
        offers lower priced food options typically than other restaurants which provide
        table service. As of Nov. 16, 2020, McDonald's has a market cap of ~$161B. As of
        Nov. 16, 2020, Starbucks has a market cap of ~$114B.
What has been the effect of 2020?
          QSR, when using Starbuck's and McDonald's as indicators, had been increasing overall
        and especially taking off in 2018. Mid-2020 industry stock prices dropped, likely due
        to COVID-19. The training predictions took time to adjust to an unexpected drop in price.
        Starbuck's and McDonald's quickly recovered from price drops and have risen higher since.
