Defense Industry


What is the Defense industry?
          The Defense industry develops and manufactures products for the U.S. Department of Defense
        and U.S. Military. Two big players in defense are Northrop Grumman Corporation and Raytheon 
        Technologies Corporation. These companies were used as market indicators because they are two of
        the top defense contractors according to Bloomberg.
What has been the effect of 2020?
          2020 has shown both companies dipping in closing prices. The training prediction was unable to
        predict the effects of COVID-19 and neither company has recovered in closing price since 2020.
        Looking at annual patterns for Q1 shows both companies drop in closing price. This may be the most
        beneficial analysis of these two stock prices. Although the drop in Q1 of 2020 was more dramatic,
        each company was already expected to drop in price for Q1.
